About the App

Melo is a lightweight music player built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a fast and focused listening experience.

Features: spinning CD, sleep timer, speed control, grouped search, keyboard shortcuts, liked songs, recently played, playlists, shuffle and repeat, mini player with progress, and theme support.
