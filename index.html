About the App

Melo is designed around presence. Instead of fast taps and endless skipping, it brings back the physical rhythm of listening — drop the needle, let it play, and stay with the track.

Most music players optimise for speed. Melo does the opposite — it slows things down, removes noise, and makes listening feel intentional again.

Why Melo exists

Modern music apps are built for speed — search, skip, repeat. That makes music feel disposable.

Melo focuses on the opposite idea: stay with the song, not the algorithm.

What makes it different

Melo isn't just visual — it's interactive. You don't just press play, you drop the tonearm. The vinyl spins. Playback feels like an action, not a button press.
